# TransferLink User Workflow Map (v2.0 Prototype) This document maps the screen sequences for each primary user role, demonstrating how the prototype facilitates a seamless, bi-directional information loop. --- ## 1. LTC Nurse Workflow: Initiating a Transfer *The primary driver of the clinical handoff process.* 1. **S0: Role Selector / S15: Login:** Nurse selects their persona (e.g., Sarah Mitchell, RN) and shift. 2. **S1: Patient List:** View the roster for "Cascade View Assisted Living". 3. **S2: Patient Record (LTC View):** Review Margaret Tanaka's baseline clinical data and person-centered care preferences. 4. **S3: Initiate Transfer:** Quick-entry form for the emergency (reason, symptoms, destination). 5. **S4: Transfer Confirmation:** Final review of the transfer record. 6. **S5: QR Code Generated:** The "Hero" screen for physical handoff. 7. **S6: Print Preview (Optional):** Generates a paper backup with the embedded QR code. --- ## 2. EMS Crew Workflow: En-Route Care *Immediate access to life-saving data during transport.* 1. **S7: QR Scanner (EMS):** Scan the physical or digital QR code provided by the LTC facility. 2. **S8: Patient Record (EMS View):** * **Priority Banner:** Immediate visibility of Code Status (DNR/DNI) and Primary Language. * **POLST Access:** Red button for instant access to the legal medical directive. * **Expanded Comfort Section:** Insights into how to manage the patient's distress during transport. --- ## 3. ED Staff Workflow: Hospital Intake & Discharge *Receiving the patient and closing the information loop.* 1. **S9: QR Scanner (ED):** Scan the patient's QR code upon arrival at the Emergency Department. 2. **S10: Patient Record (ED View):** Identical to the EMS view but with a primary action to **"Complete ED Return Info"**. 3. **S11: ED Return Form:** ED Physician/Nurse documents the diagnosis, vitals at discharge, and instructions. 4. **S12: Transfer Complete:** Confirmation that the data has been synced back to the originating LTC facility. --- ## 4. Facility Admin & Charge Nurse Workflow: Operational Oversight *Monitoring facility-wide safety and return notifications.* 1. **S17: Facility Dashboard:** Real-time stats on active transfers, pending returns, and critical alerts. 2. **S18: Transfer History:** Audit log of all past movements to track facility outcomes. 3. **S19: SBAR Handoff Report:** Generate a standardized clinical report for any resident for shift changes or follow-ups. 4. **S13: Facility Return Record:** Notification and review of the ED data once the patient is back at the facility. 5. **S14: Transfer Timeline:** A vertical audit trail of every scan and update throughout the journey. --- ## 5. Patient Admission Workflow: Onboarding *Preparing the baseline for future emergencies.* 1. **S1: Add Patient Button:** Triggers the 5-step Intake Modal. 2. **S26-S14: Intake Steps:** Guided capture of Demographics, Clinical Baseline, Contacts, and Care Preferences. 3. **S2: New Record:** The patient is now "Emergency Ready".
